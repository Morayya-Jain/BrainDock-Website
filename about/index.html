About BrainDock

BrainDock is an AI-powered focus assistant that helps you stay on task during work and study sessions. It uses your webcam to detect distractions - without ever storing video or using facial recognition.

Our Mission

We believe deep focus is a skill that can be trained. BrainDock was built to give people a gentle, intelligent companion that notices when attention drifts - so you can build stronger focus habits over time.

Privacy First

No video is stored. No facial recognition. No screenshots saved. BrainDock processes everything in real-time and discards it immediately. Your focus data stays on your device unless you choose to sync it.

macOS & Windows

BrainDock runs natively on macOS and Windows as a lightweight desktop app. It works quietly in the background during your sessions - no browser extensions or plugins needed.
