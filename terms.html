2. Description of Service

BrainDock is a focus tracking application designed to help users monitor their study or work sessions. The Application:

  • Uses your device's camera to detect your presence and potential distractions
  • Monitors your active window and browser URLs to detect distraction apps/websites
  • Optionally captures screenshots for AI analysis (disabled by default, user-controlled)
  • Sends camera frames and/or screenshots to third-party AI services for real-time analysis
  • Generates session reports summarising your focus patterns
  • Does NOT perform facial recognition, identity verification, or biometric identification

2.1 What BrainDock Does

  • Detects whether a person is present at the desk
  • Detects whether you appear to be using distraction devices (phones, tablets, game controllers, etc.)
  • Monitors active window titles and browser URLs to detect distraction apps/websites
  • Optional AI Screenshot Analysis: If enabled by you, captures screenshots and sends them to AI for distraction detection (disabled by default)
  • Generates focus session statistics and PDF reports
  • Provides AI-generated insights about your focus patterns

2.2 What BrainDock Does NOT Do

  • No Facial Recognition: We do not identify who you are
  • No Biometric Templates: We do not create, store, or match biometric data
  • No Identity Verification: We do not verify or match your identity across sessions
  • No Video Recording: We do not save or store video footage
  • No Screenshot Storage: Screenshots (if enabled) are processed in real-time and not saved
  • No Continuous Surveillance: Processing only occurs during active sessions you initiate
  • No Keystroke Logging: We do not record what you type
