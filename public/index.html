Features

Timed Sessions

Start a timed session whenever you're ready. BrainDock observes your focus in real-time when you are working.

Attention Coach

Gently notices when devices pull your attention away or when you step away from your desk.

Screen Awareness

Optionally tracks the active window to identify shifts to distracting applications or websites.

Performance Report

Generate detailed PDF reports with insights into your session highlighting your focus scores, distractions & activity logs.

How It Works

Three simple steps to better focus.

1

Download the App

Click the Download button for your operating system (macOS, Windows, Linux). Configure your settings and you're good to go!

2

Start a Session

Click Start to begin your session, align your camera view, and start working. BrainDock runs quietly in the background. You can pause or stop any time.

3

Report Generated

A PDF report containing session insights and analytics is available after each session ends.
