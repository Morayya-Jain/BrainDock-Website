2. Description of Service

BrainDock is a focus assistance application designed to help users observe their study or work sessions. The Application:

  • Uses your device's camera to identify your presence and potential distractions
  • Observes your active window and browser URLs to identify distraction apps/websites
  • Optionally captures screenshots for AI analysis (disabled by default, user-controlled)
  • Sends camera frames and/or screenshots to third-party AI services for real-time analysis
  • Generates session reports summarising your focus patterns
  • Provides an online account and dashboard to view session history and manage settings
  • Does NOT perform facial recognition, identity verification, or biometric identification

2.1 What BrainDock Does

  • Identifies whether a person is present at the desk
  • Identifies whether you appear to be using distraction devices (phones, tablets, game controllers, etc.)
  • Observes active window titles and browser URLs to identify distraction apps/websites
  • Optional AI Screenshot Analysis: If enabled by you, captures screenshots and sends them to AI for distraction identification (disabled by default)
  • Generates focus session statistics and PDF reports
  • Provides AI-generated insights about your focus patterns

2.2 What BrainDock Does NOT Do

  • No Facial Recognition: We do not identify who you are
  • No Biometric Templates: We do not create, store, or match biometric data
  • No Identity Verification: We do not verify or match your identity across sessions
  • No Video Storage: We do not save or store video footage
  • No Screenshot Storage: Screenshots (if enabled) are processed in real-time and not permanently saved
  • No Continuous Observation: Processing only occurs during active sessions you initiate
  • No Keystroke Logging: We do not capture what you type

    • Google Gemini (servers located in the United States)
  • These providers may retain data as per their respective policies
  • We do not store raw camera frames on your device or our servers

7.2 Screen Awareness

  • The Application observes your active window titles and browser URLs to identify distraction apps/websites
  • This data is processed locally on your device and compared against your list
  • Window titles and URLs are NOT sent to third-party services (unless AI Screenshot Analysis is enabled)

7.3 Optional AI Screenshot Analysis

  • This feature is disabled by default and requires you to explicitly enable it

7.4 Session Data

7.6 Account and Device Data
